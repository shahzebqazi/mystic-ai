To expose machine-readable results, enable JSON in your instance config under search.formats, then call /search?q=…&format=json 1. Pair with a reverse proxy for CORS and rate limits 2.

Typical Docker setups mount settings.yml so you can toggle engines and formats without rebuilding 3.
